HERMIONE

Hermione Granger is a central protagonist in the Harry Potter series, widely celebrated as the "brightest witch of her age" for her extraordinary intellect and mastery of magic. Born to Muggle parents, she enters Hogwarts with a fierce dedication to her studies, often using her encyclopedic knowledge and logical reasoning to save her best friends, Harry Potter and Ron Weasley, from countless perilous situations. While initially perceived by peers as an "insufferable know-it-all," her character is defined by a deep moral compass and unwavering loyalty. She famously champions the rights of the marginalized, such as house-elves through her organization S.P.E.W., and repeatedly risks her life in the fight against Lord Voldemort.
